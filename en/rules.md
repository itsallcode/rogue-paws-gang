## Rolls

**Basic Roll**: Roll 1d6, must roll **equal to or lower** than your stat to succeed.

**Advantage**: Roll 2+ d6s, use the **lowest** result.

**Disadvantage**: Roll 2+ d6s, use the **highest** result.

**Failure**: GM decides if failure costs luck points (1-3) based on the story situation.

GM can impose advantage or disadvantage depending on what the character tries to do.

## Luck

**Starting/Maximum Luck**: 6 (9 for cats)

**Losing Luck**:

1-3 points per failure depending on severity and situation

**Regaining Luck**:
- Good in-character roleplaying: +1-2 points (GM discretion)
- Short rest (10 minutes): +2 points
- Full night's sleep: restore to maximum

**Zero Luck**:

Character becomes unconscious/incapacitated (GM chooses how). Can only be restored through friends' roleplay efforts.

**Special Cat Rule**:

GM can invoke "curiosity killed the cat" to compel investigation of dangerous/interesting things.
